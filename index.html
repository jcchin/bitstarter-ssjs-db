We send out facebook invitations when we want to throw a party...


But how do we decide on the type of party in the first place?

The Problem:
Collaborative event brainstorming is tough. Juggling text messages among multiple friends is cumbersome, ideas get lost in long email chains, and it's hard to fit everyone's schedule.

The Solution: Meet Huddle.
The new think tank for events. We've grabbed all the best attributes of multiple communications tools and smashed them together so you can get together with more friends, more frequently.

FAQ

The last thing the world needs is another social networking app, why are you making this?
We agree! That's why we are trying to make something that feels more like
a communcation tool, such as email, or a group texting service.
And we're specially tailoring it for iterative and collaborative event planning.
Hasn't this already been done?
Not well! Most people still use a cluttered combination of email, texting, facebook, and voting sites to coordinate event ideas with friends. Now there is a single centralized way to brainstorm and agree on plans.
